What is Biblical Counseling?

Biblical counseling is based on a Scriptural understanding of human behavior, focusing on the heart as the core of all thoughts, emotions, and actions, in line with Proverbs 4:23, which states, "from it flow the issues of life." A biblical counselor, who is also a fellow believer, works alongside you to address your struggles, ensuring that the Triune God of the Bible is central to the solutions and guidance provided. This approach integrates faith, relying on God's wisdom and power for personal growth and transformation.

In contrast, psychological counseling is grounded in human observation and studies of behavior, without a spiritual dimension. It focuses primarily on the mind and emotions from a secular perspective. Solutions are limited to the horizontal realm of human relationships and self-understanding, lacking the vertical connection to God that is central to biblical counseling. As a result, while psychological counseling can offer helpful insights, it does not engage with the deeper spiritual aspect of healing and transformation.

Types of Counselling

Pre Marital Counseling

Building a life together? Pre marital counseling will help you and your partner align on beliefs, values, and expectations, laying the groundwork for a strong, lasting marriage.

All of us spend between 5-8 years preparing for our professional lives which may change after a few years, but hardly any time is devoted to the preparation for a lifetime commitment with our life-partner! BCTI offers personalized counselling / coaching to prepare you for this life-long commitment. Topics include God's design for marriage, discovering your identity, dealing with differences, quality communication, managing conflicts, dealing with finances, in-laws etc.

Couples Counseling

Struggling in your marriage? Couples invariably face issues related to differences, communications, expectations etc after the honeymoon period is over. Our couples counseling is designed to help you resolve conflicts and gain new perspectives that strengthen your relationship.

Family Counseling

Facing challenges within the family? Our family counseling addresses emotional, behavioral, and relational dynamics to help bring peace and harmony into your home.
