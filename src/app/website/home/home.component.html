Counselling

Feeling worn out, confused, burnt out, or just plain tired? We're here for you. Come share your story with us, and we will offer you a nurturing environment where you can share your concerns and struggles. In addition, we provide guidance and truth rooted in love to help you navigate through life's challenges and find clarity and relief.

Training

Enhance your skills with our specialised courses in English and Hindi. Whether you're a layperson seeking personal growth, a church leader, or a pastor, our training programs are tailored to equip you with practical tools and knowledge. We focus on providing comprehensive instruction to help you apply Biblical principles in real-life situations and make a meaningful impact in your community.
