Frequently Asked Questions

Biblical counselling is grounded in a Scriptural understanding of human behaviour, focusing on the heart from which flows the issues of life (Proverbs 4:23).

A Biblical counsellor, a fellow believer in Christ, stands by you to address your life struggles, always involving the Triune God of the Bible in the solutions.

In contrast, psychological counseling is grounded in human observation and studies of behavior, without a spiritual dimension. It focuses primarily on the mind and emotions from a secular perspective. Solutions are limited to the horizontal realm of human relationships and self-understanding, lacking the vertical connection to God that is central to biblical counseling. As a result, while psychological counseling can offer helpful insights, it does not engage with the deeper spiritual aspect of healing and transformation.

All of us spend between 5-8 years preparing for our professional lives which may change after a few years, but hardly any time is devoted to the preparation for a lifetime commitment with our life-partner! BCTI offers personalized counselling / coaching to prepare you for this life-long commitment. Topics include God's design for marriage, discovering your identity, dealing with differences, quality communication, managing conflicts, dealing with finances, in-laws etc.
