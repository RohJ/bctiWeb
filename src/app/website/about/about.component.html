Our Story

The Biblical Counseling Trust of India (BCTI), founded by Dr.John K. John in 2005, trains pastors and church leaders to provide guidance for modern challenges. Initially focused on North India, BCTI follows the Biblical Counseling model of the Christian Counseling and Educational Foundation (CCEF). BCTI offer an 'Introduction to Biblical Counseling Certificate' after completing four modules: How People Change, Instruments in the Redeemer’s Hands, Health and Healing, and Marriage and Parenting. Courses are available via Zoom in Hindi and English, with CCEF's English courses online. BCTI also provides counseling in English and Hindi. Our office is in Dwarka, New Delhi.

Our Vision

Empowering local Churches & Ministries

Strengthen local churches and ministries by providing them with the resources and support they need to thrive and fulfill their mission in the community. Empower leaders to create lasting, positive change through Christ-centered service.

Impacting and healing broken communities

Bring hope and restoration to broken communities by addressing their spiritual and physical needs. Through compassionate outreach, foster healing and unity grounded in love and faith.

Equipping Christians to work as the Body of Christ

Equip Christians with the tools, knowledge, and encouragement to work together as one Body in Christ. Enable them to serve effectively, building up the Church and advancing God’s Kingdom

Building communities founded on the Word of God

Build strong, resilient communities rooted in the unchanging truth of God's Word. Through discipleship and biblical teaching, create a foundation of faith that transforms lives and relationships.
