Counselor Mentoring Program

What is BCTI Counselor Mentoring?
  • The BCTI Counselor Mentoring Program is designed to (help) grow counselors and interested counselors in their understanding as well as practical knowledge of biblical counseling.
  • In this program, the counselor/ interested counselor will be paired with a mentor for a period of 4 months.
Who is a mentor?

Mentors are part of the BCTI Team and will provide the necessary support required in this process.

Who is a mentee?

Mentees are BCTI Alumni who have completed the online courses How People Change and Instrumnent's In The Reddemer's Hands OR The CCEF courses Dynamics of Biblical Change and Helping Relationships. This is for those want to be involved in counseling or who are already involved in counseling, either formal or informal.
