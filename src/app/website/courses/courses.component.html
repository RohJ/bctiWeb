Equipping Christians to be Ambassadors for Christ

These are courses aimed at equipping Christians to help people navigate the challenges and struggles of modern Christian living.

Anu's Story

Anu is a homemaker with 4 children in Kerala. She was introduced to BCTI by her sister. Through the online courses conducted by BCTI, she experienced growth and transformation. This equipped her to navigate her marriage and parenting in a godly way as she matured in her understanding and faith.

English Courses

BCTI follows the Biblical Counseling model of the Christian Counseling and Educational Foundation (CCEF). We offer an 'Introduction to Biblical Counseling Certificate' after completing four modules:

  • How People Change
  • Instruments in the Redeemer's Hands
  • Health and Healing
  • Marriage and Parenting

Interactive Courses are available via Zoom in English.

CCEF-BCTI Courses

BCTI partners with the Christian Counseling & Educational Foundation (CCEF), Philadelphia, USA, (https://www.ccef.org/) to provide biblical counselling courses in India at a discounted rate.

These online courses provide a solid biblical foundation for understanding God, yourself, and others, making them invaluable for anyone involved in ministry or counselling. The following two courses are offered:

  • Dynamics of Biblical Change
  • Helping Relationships
