BCTI follows the Biblical Counseling model of the Christian Counseling and Educational Foundation (CCEF). CCEF-BCTI Courses

BCTI partners with the Christian Counseling & Educational Foundation (CCEF)* to provide biblical counselling courses in India at a discounted rate.

These online courses provide a solid biblical foundation for understanding God, yourself, and others, making them invaluable for anyone involved in ministry or counselling.
